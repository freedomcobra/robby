A Little About Me

I am a graphic designer from Louisville Kentucky. I graduated in 2012 from Western Kentucky University with a major in advertisement and graphic design. I am a professional graphic designer with a passion for print and conventional art. With each completed piece in my portfolio came a new challenge and many problems to solve; as a result, I have developed a tenacity at completing the job efficiently and a focus on constant improvement.
